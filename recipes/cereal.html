Cereal

Description

I learned how to make cereal with my mother-in-law's wonderful granola recipe. She's made it for years. It's so flexible and so much cheaper (and better) than store-bought granola. Store in airtight containers.

Ingredients

  • 7 cups quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup wheat germ
  • 1 cup wheat bran
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup honey
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped dates
  • 1 cup flaked coconut (Optional)

Steps

    Preheat the oven to 275 degrees F (135 degrees C). Mix oats, wheat germ, and wheat bran together in a large bowl. Stir brown sugar, vegetable oil, honey, and water together in another bowl until blended. Mix in vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Pour brown sugar mixture into oat mixture and stir until evenly moist. Transfer to a large, shallow baking dish. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 15 minutes, until lightly browned, about 45 minutes. Add dates, pecans, and coconut to the baking dish and stir to combine. Continue to bake for about 15 more minutes. Remove from the oven and cool to room temperature.
